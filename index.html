About Ian

    My name is Ian Deans. I'm not the Canadian politician Ian Deans; Nor am I the Canadian professional skier Ian Deans; I'm actually not even Canadian. This Ian Deans, is a technologist and science enthusiast from California.

I have experience with various frameworks and libraries as well as exposure to modern design patterns and workflows. I tend to be more comfortable on the server side of things but I also enjoy building on the front-end.

Education

Learner's Guild

    Learner's Guild is a 40 week apprenticeship program in Oakland, CA. It is a place where people with some experience in programming can take on web development as a new skill, or refine existing ones. Focused on building full-stack applications in Node.js in a professional environment. Learner's Guild uses weekly development cycles paired with code shipping goals and deadlines to drive apprentices to learn through experience. There are on-site and remote professionals that lead a couple of open source projects as well as offer assistance and workshops on various technologies and practices. Here apprentices learn not only to build and ship quality code while in team, but also how to assertively give and take feedback as a way to improve team relations, efficiency, and productivity.

  • Was lead coach to multiple teams of apprentices
  • Designed, developed, and deployed apps to live production
